# I-Corps Mentor Preparation

## Overview

Preparing to become a mentor for the NSF I-Corps Hub: Mid-Atlantic Region program. This came from meeting with Glenn Hellman on 2026-02-02.

**What is I-Corps?**
The NSF Innovation Corps program focuses on "derisking commercialization through world-class customer discovery training and support." It helps entrepreneurs and innovation teams develop their technologies into viable commercial ventures.

**Mentor Role:**
- Provide professional industry experience and practical business guidance
- Help teams refine their customer discovery approach
- Share networks and connections within the entrepreneurial community
- Support teams in developing stronger business models
