### Major Strategic Shift: Sprint-First Positioning

**What Changed:** Complete repositioning of the AI Workflow Sprint from a complementary offering to the primary go-to-market focus. The Sprint is now positioned as the natural entry point for organizations.

**Why It Matters:**
- Solves the "knowing-doing gap" - traditional training teaches skills but doesn't guarantee application
- Teams build working workflows they use immediately (not just learn features)
- Lower risk entry point ($3,500 vs $5,000) that creates organic pathways to additional services
- Naturally identifies team champions and reveals skill gaps that justify deeper training
